Process

Simple coordination for everyday local support.

HelpNear keeps the core flow clear: request help, connect with nearby helpers, coordinate, complete, and review.

1

Create a local request

Describe what you need, choose a category, and share enough context for nearby helpers to respond.

2

Discover nearby helpers

Browse local profiles, availability, categories, and map-based discovery when location access is enabled.

3

Send or accept offers

Helpers can send offers. Requesters can review offers and accept the one that fits the need.

4

Coordinate in messages

Once matched, both sides can coordinate through in-app messaging.

5

Mark completed

After the interaction, the requester can mark the request completed.

6

Leave a review

Reviews are tied to completed requests to support trust and accountability.

Safety

Trust and safety are part of the beta design.

HelpNear is designed for everyday community support, not emergency response. The beta includes safety reporting and moderation support to review concerns.

  • ✓User profiles help people understand who they are connecting with.
  • ✓Reviews appear after completed request interactions.
  • ✓Reports can be submitted from key safety entry points.
  • ✓Admin moderation support is available during private beta.
Limitations

Private beta scope.

These limitations are intentional while the app is tested with a small group.

1
Android only

iOS is not available during this beta phase.

2
In-app notifications only

Native push notifications are not enabled yet.

3
No payment system

HelpNear does not process payments in the current build.

4
Email/password sign-in

Google and Facebook sign-in are disabled for now.

Emergency disclaimer: HelpNear is not an emergency service. For emergencies or immediate safety risks, contact local emergency services directly.
FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Clear answers for early testers and community members.

What is HelpNear?

HelpNear is a local helper and community support marketplace that helps people request support, offer help, and coordinate safely with nearby community members.

Is HelpNear available on iOS?

No. HelpNear is currently Android only during private beta. iOS support may be considered later.

Does HelpNear handle payments?

No. HelpNear does not include a payment system in the current private beta build.

Is HelpNear an emergency service?

No. HelpNear is not an emergency service. For emergencies or immediate safety risks, contact local emergency services directly.

How do I report a safety concern?

Use the in-app reporting options or contact support at helpnear.support@gmail.com.

How can I request account or data deletion?

Send a request to helpnear.support@gmail.com. Include the email address associated with your HelpNear account.

Interested in the private beta?

Contact HelpNear support to ask about beta access, share feedback, or request account and data deletion.
